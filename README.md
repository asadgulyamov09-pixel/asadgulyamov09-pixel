🧠 Biggest Lesson So Far

One of the biggest lessons I have learned is that cybersecurity is much more than technology.

Firewalls, MFA, backups, cloud security, and security tools are important, but they only become effective when supported by strong governance, clear ownership, risk-based decision making, and alignment with business objectives.

Strong cybersecurity programs require:

- Effective Governance
- Clear Ownership
- Risk-Based Decision Making
- Business Alignment
- Continuous Improvement
- Accountability

Technology protects systems.

Governance protects organizations.
